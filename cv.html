Jan Van den Bussche

Brief cv

In 1989 I graduated from the University of Antwerp (UIA) with a diploma in Mathematics - Computer Science. I started to work on a PhD in Computer Science at the same university, under Jan Paredaens, with a four-year scholarship from the Belgian National Fund for Scientific Research. In 1993 I obtained the PhD degree with "highest distinction and special felicitations by the jury". My thesis was awarded with the IBM-Belgium prize for PhD dissertations in computer science. I spent the academic year 1994-95 at INRIA Rocquencourt in the Verso group of Serge Abiteboul, with a post-doc fellowship from the European Commission. Back in Belgium I returned to UIA on a post-doctoral research position from the Belgian National Fund for Scientific Research. In September 1996 I became a faculty member here at LUC. I spent the summer of 1999 at UCSD.
